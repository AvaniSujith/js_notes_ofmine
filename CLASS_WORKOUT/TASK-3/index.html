Caesar Cipher Instructions

What is Encryption?

Encryption is the process of converting normal text into coded text to protect information. The Caesar cipher is one of the oldest and simplest forms of encrypting text.

Available Methods

1. Normal Shift Encryption

  1. Enter your message in the input field
  2. Choose how many positions to shift the alphabet(1-25)
  3. Click "Encrypt" to see your encoded message

2. Shuffled Encryption

  1. Enter your message in the input field
  2. Click "Encrypt" to encode using the shuffled alphabet

Shuffled Alphabet Reference:

Original: ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
Shuffled: QAZWSXCDERFVBGTHYNUJIOKMLP
